About Us

Founded in 2024 Solartech Renewable Solutions Ltd is driven by the belief that the shift to clean energy is both essential and economically empowering.

We specialize in reliable, cost-effective renewable energy systems for residential, commercial, and public sectors. Our expert engineers and technicians design tailored solutions that maximize efficiency and reduce carbon footprints—powered by top-tier products like PV panels, inverters, lithium batteries, and more.

Beyond solar, we offer advanced power quality systems and a lineup of electric vehicles (EVs) ushering in a smarter, greener way to move.

As a trusted distributor of global brands including Deye, Dyness, Longi, Nruit and Feeo, our systems are built to withstand diverse environments and deliver exceptional performance. We handle everything from system design to post-installation monitoring, with round-the-clock support to keep your energy flowing.

Our Mission

To deliver safe, sustainable, and user-friendly energy solutions—making clean power accessible for everyone through innovation, service, and a commitment to the environment.

Our Vision

A future where green energy improves lives and preserves the planet. We go beyond systems—developing smart, intuitive solutions that simplify energy management and inspire a more sustainable world.
